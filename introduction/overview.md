Our care plans and packages are designed to ensure your website remains functional, secure, and optimized at all times.

Here’s what each plan includes regardless what Care Plan tier you subscribed to:

| **Includes**               | **Details**                                                                                  |
|----------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
